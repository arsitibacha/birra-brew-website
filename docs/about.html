About us

Built around slow coffee, soft light, and people who care about details.

Why we opened Birra Brew

Birra Brew started as a simple idea: a small, bright space where the only focus is coffee and people. We wanted a place where you can see everything – the bar, the grinders, the steam – and feel calm at the same time.

We keep our menu short, our design minimal, and our team small. That lets us pay attention to every shot and every guest.

How we work

Every morning, we dial in espresso, taste filter brews, and adjust as the day changes. We believe in transparency, traceable sourcing, and paying producers fairly.

We’re always learning – from other cafés we admire, from our roasters, and from the people who sit at our bar each day.
